Dear Clients,

Welcome to my practice. I appreciate the opportunity to work with you over the phone to support your growth and development. I look forward to starting our journey together.

My preferred method of payment for phone sessions is through a PayPal account. Paypal is free and easy to set up. You can use credit cards, ATM cards, debit cards, or a bank account as your payment source. In order to ease the transition, I am providing these instructions. If you have any difficulties setting up your account, please let me know.

To Set Up An Account:

  • Go to www.paypal.com
  • Click on Sign Up
  • Choose Personal Account
  • Enter Demographics, Choose Password, Follow the prompts
  • You will receive an email from PayPal asking you to confirm your account

To Send Money:

  • Log In
  • Click on Send Money
  • Enter doctor_hayl@yahoo.com under recipients email
  • Follow the prompts and choose payment source

To Add Bank Account:

  • Click on Profile
  • Click on Bank Accounts
  • Enter Banking Information
